Concept D

Story-first

Today's market is told as 3–4 short chapters. Each story explains a theme; stocks appear as evidence, not the headline.

Mobile + desktop · scroll narrative
Dashboard · What's Moving · Discover Stocks

Three stories are moving the market today

Read the themes first. Tap a stock when you want the full picture.

Story 1 · Leading theme

AI demand is lifting chip stocks again

Investors are betting that big tech will keep spending on data centers. When that happens, companies that make chips often rise together—even if you do not follow every earnings report.

NVDA +1.98%
AMD +1.12%
Story 2

Oil is firm, and energy names are catching up

When oil prices hold steady, energy producers often benefit. Think of it as the market pricing in steady fuel demand—not a single company's surprise.

XOM +1.18%
CVX +0.84%
Story 3 · Caution

Banks are drifting lower as yields ease

When interest rates expectations fall, banks can lag. Money is rotating into tech instead—a common “risk-on” pattern beginners often miss.

JPM −0.41%

Design intent

  • Page answers: “What is the market talking about?” before “which tickers?”
  • Stories map to MOVER_STORIES / session narrative engine—not a sortable list
  • Deep Dive opens from chip “Why →” only; no rank column, no % leaderboard hero
Concept E

Sector-first

Organize by where companies live in the economy. Users learn which industries are winning or losing, then meet representative stocks.

Sector map · tap sector to expand (v2)
Dashboard · What's Moving

Who's leading today?

Sectors group similar businesses. When a sector moves, it usually means a shared story—not luck on one stock.

Leading

Technology & Energy are carrying the session. Money is flowing into growth and commodities.

Lagging

Financials are soft. Investors are less excited about banks when yields drift down.

Technology Leading

AI spending hopes are lifting chip and platform names.

In focus: NVDA · META · MSFT

Energy Leading

Oil is steady; producers are bid alongside the broad market.

In focus: XOM · CVX

Healthcare Quiet

Defensive names are flat—no dominant headline today.

Watching: JNJ · UNH

Financials Lagging

Lower rate expectations pressure bank margins.

In focus: JPM · BAC

Design intent

  • Teaches sector vocabulary passively (Technology, Energy, etc.)
  • “Leading / Lagging / Quiet” replaces Gainers/Losers tabs
  • Tap sector → expand story + 2–4 stocks; tap symbol → Deep Dive
Concept F

Beginner-first briefing

Start with a 30-second plain-English brief: what's going well, what to watch out for. Stocks appear only after context lands.

Briefing → opportunities / risks → stocks behind the story
Dashboard · What's Moving

Today's 30-second brief

The market is upbeat, led by tech and energy—but banks are slipping.

That usually means investors feel okay taking risk (tech) while still caring about oil (energy). When banks lag, it often signals lower interest-rate expectations—not panic.

Opportunities today

  • Chip makers riding AI demand
  • Energy names if oil stays firm

Risks to know

  • Banks sensitive to rate cuts
  • Big tech already up—moves can reverse fast

Design intent

  • Zero finance jargon above the fold: no “movers”, “session”, “breadth”
  • Opportunity / Risk framing teaches how to read a day without predicting returns
  • Each stock row explains role in the story, not rank or %
